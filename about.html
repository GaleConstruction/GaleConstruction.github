About

Established in 1986, we are professionals in the construction industry with a long track record of providing quality workmanship at reasonable prices.

Located in Oakville Ontario, Gale Construction provides quality general construction and specializes in custom homes, chimney repair, masonry, brick work, stone parging, tuck pointing, flagstone, blocks, fireplaces, cultured stone, and glass block.
